Privacy Policy

At Bombon, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app or visit our website. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Please take a moment to review it carefully.

Effective Date: July 26, 2024

Information We Collect


We collect the following types of information to enhance your experience and provide our services:

  • Personal Information: This includes data that can identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, financial data (like account balances and transactions), and any other details you voluntarily provide when registering or using our app.

  • Non-Personal Information: We also gather technical information such as your IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, and usage data. This helps us improve app performance, troubleshoot issues, and personalize your experience.



Data Security


We employ industry-standard security protocols to protect your data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. While no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.



Cookies and Tracking Technologies


Bombon uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide a personalized user experience. These tools help us analyze how you use our app and website. You can manage your cookie preferences directly in your browser settings.



Your Rights and Choices


You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and Update: You can view, correct, or update your personal information at any time through the app or by contacting our support team.

  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal requirements.

  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing emails and notifications at any time via your account settings or by following the unsubscribe instructions in the communication.

  • Cookie Preferences: You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.



Updates to This Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, with the revised effective date clearly indicated.



Contact Us


If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or if you need assistance with managing your information, please contact us at support@bombon.com.

What is Bombon?

Bombon is a finance management application designed to help individuals and businesses track, manage, and optimize their finances. With features such as budgeting, expense tracking, bill reminders, investment monitoring, and more, it simplifies your financial management and helps you stay on top of your financial goals.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store, then follow the sign-up prompts. You’ll need to provide an email address, create a password, and set up some basic information about your finances to get started.

Is my data safe?

Yes, your privacy and data security are our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your financial data. We also comply with all applicable regulations to ensure your information is safe and secure.

How do I track my expenses?

You can manually enter expenses or have them automatically imported from your linked bank accounts. The app categorizes each expense (e.g., groceries, dining, transportation) to help you understand where your money is going. You can also set spending limits for each category.

How do I set up a budget?

To create a budget, go to the "Budget" section of the app, select the categories you want to track (e.g., food, entertainment, utilities), and set a spending limit for each category. The app will notify you when you're close to exceeding your budget for any category.

Can I set up bill reminders?

Yes! Bombon allows you to set reminders for upcoming bills and due dates. You'll receive notifications ahead of time so you never miss a payment. You can set reminders for credit cards, utilities, loans, subscriptions, and more.
