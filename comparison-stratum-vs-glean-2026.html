Bottom Line

Stratum AI and Glean solve fundamentally different problems. Stratum AI is a vertical solution for revenue teams - optimizing ad spend, unifying customer data, and automating marketing with safety guardrails. Glean is a horizontal solution for all employees - finding information, automating any workflow, and augmenting work with AI.

They can actually be complementary: use Stratum AI for your marketing and revenue operations while using Glean for company-wide knowledge search and general workflow automation.
