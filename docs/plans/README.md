## FAQ

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade anytime. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full features.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Your data is retained according to your new plan's retention period. Export before downgrading if needed.

**Q: Can I add more ad accounts without upgrading?**
A: Additional ad accounts can be purchased as add-ons. Contact sales for pricing.

**Q: Do you offer annual billing?**
A: Yes, annual billing includes a 20% discount.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fees for Starter and Professional. Enterprise may include onboarding services.
