What Smartly is great at

  • Cross‑channel creative + media workflow from one platform across major ad channels.
  • Creative automation and personalization at scale (studio‑style production workflows).
  • Creative prediction features (forecasting creative effectiveness) to reduce guesswork pre‑launch.
Use this framing: Smartly = creative ops + orchestration. If the prospect is bottlenecked by creative production, Smartly is a strong fit.

How Stratum wins (talk track)

  • Trust‑Gated Autopilot: validate signal health before automation executes.
  • Inventory protection: stop spend when products are out‑of‑stock — automatically.
  • Decision safety: approvals, decision logs, rollback (reduce “automation anxiety”).
  • Performance layer: agents optimize pacing/bids/audiences + anomaly detection.
One‑liner: “Smartly helps you build and ship creative faster. Stratum makes sure automations are safe and profitable — even when tracking or inventory is messy.”

Objection → response

  • “We already automate in Smartly.” → “Perfect — Stratum’s job is to prevent expensive wrong moves when pixels/attribution/inventory are unreliable.”
  • “Creative speed is our priority.” → “Great. Use Smartly for creative ops; Stratum sits above as the trust layer that decides when to scale, pause, or shift budget.”
  • “We need cross‑channel control.” → “We do cross‑platform optimization plus trust gating and inventory sync.”

Trap questions (to reveal gaps)

  • “If Meta CAPI breaks for 24 hours, what stops your automations from scaling the wrong ads?”
  • “Can you auto‑pause spend when the top SKU is out‑of‑stock — without manual rules?”
  • “Do you have a signal health score that gates every automated change?”
  • “Can we audit every automated action and roll it back?”
If they say “we can do that with rules,” respond: “Rules don’t know whether the signal is trustworthy — they just execute.”
