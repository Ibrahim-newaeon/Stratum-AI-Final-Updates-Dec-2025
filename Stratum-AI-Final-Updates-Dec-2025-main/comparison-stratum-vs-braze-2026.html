The Bottom Line

Stratum AI and Braze are complementary, not competing platforms. They serve different parts of the marketing funnel:


Stratum AI excels at customer acquisition - optimizing paid advertising, unifying customer data, providing advanced attribution, and ensuring safe automation with its unique trust-gated approach.


Braze excels at customer retention & engagement - delivering personalized messages across email, push, SMS, and in-app channels with sophisticated journey orchestration.


For a complete marketing stack, many organizations could benefit from using both: Stratum AI for acquisition and revenue operations, feeding high-quality customer data to Braze for lifecycle engagement.
