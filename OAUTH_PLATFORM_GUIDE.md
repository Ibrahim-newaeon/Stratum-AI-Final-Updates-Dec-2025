### TikTok-Specific Considerations

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Business Center** | Required - similar to Meta Business Manager |
| **Advertiser vs BC** | User may have access to multiple advertisers under one BC |
| **Market Restrictions** | Some features not available in all countries |
| **Events API** | Separate setup from OAuth (needs pixel ID + access token) |
| **Approval Time** | New apps may take 1-3 days for approval |
