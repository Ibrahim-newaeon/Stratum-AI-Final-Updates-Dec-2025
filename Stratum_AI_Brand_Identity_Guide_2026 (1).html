11 ‚Äî Usage Guidelines

Do's and Don'ts

Quick reference for maintaining brand consistency across all communications.

‚úì We Are

  • Confident, not arrogant
  • Technical, but accessible
  • Transparent about AI capabilities
  • Enterprise-grade and professional
  • Trustworthy and reliable
  • Action-oriented and decisive
  • Outcome-focused with specifics
  • Supportive of human expertise

‚úó We Are NOT

  • Overpromising or hype-driven
  • Jargon-heavy or exclusionary
  • Black-box or mysterious
  • Startup-casual or gimmicky
  • Aggressive or salesy
  • Passive or theoretical
  • Vague with unsubstantiated claims
  • Claiming to replace humans

Writing Quick Reference

Element Guideline
Sentence Length Short to medium. Punchy. Avoid run-on sentences.
Vocabulary Business-grade, not academic. Clear over clever.
Pronouns "You" focused, not "We" heavy. Make the reader the hero.
Numbers Use specifics: "3x faster" not "much faster"
CTAs Action verbs: "See how", "Get started", "Book a demo"
Avoid Buzzwords: "synergy", "leverage", "paradigm shift", "disrupt"
Embrace Concrete terms: "audit trail", "explainable", "governance"
